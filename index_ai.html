Generative AI

Generative AI (GenAI) is a subset of artificial intelligence that focuses on creating new content, such as text, images, music, and more. It uses machine learning algorithms to analyze existing data and generate new, original content based on that data. GenAI has applications in various fields, including art, entertainment, marketing, and even scientific research.
